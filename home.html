Our Story

Cozy Coffee Shop started as a small passion project with one goal: to create a warm space where people could enjoy great coffee and even better moments.

From handcrafted espresso drinks to freshly baked cakes, every item on our menu is made with care, love, and quality ingredients. We believe coffee is more than a drink — it’s an experience.
